Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Baked to perfection, it’s hearty, flavorful, and a comfort food favorite around the world.

Steps to make :-

  • Cook the meat sauce – Sauté onions and garlic, add ground meat, then mix in tomato sauce and herbs. Simmer until thick.
  • Prepare the cheese mixture – Combine ricotta cheese, egg, and some grated parmesan for a creamy filling.
  • Layer the lasagna – In a baking dish, alternate layers of sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella
  • Bake the lasagna – Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25–30 minutes, then uncover and bake 10 more minutes until golden and bubbly.
